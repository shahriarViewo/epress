- text-gray-500 (medium-light gray) - CONSIDER CHANGING TO BLACK

- border-gray-100, border-gray-200 (border grays) - OK for borders
- divide-gray-100 (divider grays) - OK for dividers

RECOMMENDATIONS:
1. Most text-gray-900 instances are acceptable as they're very close to black
2. Priority changes needed for text-gray-600, text-gray-500, and text-gray-400 for better readability
3. Focus on high-traffic areas first: cart, checkout, product pages, and contact form
4. Review gray backgrounds and borders to ensure they don't affect text contrast
5. Consider accessibility standards for text contrast ratios
